Tiramisu

Description

Tiramisu is an italian recipe made of ladyfingers and a mix made of cream, eggs, mascarpone cheese, coffee and some other ingredients.

Tiramisu has a sweet flavor with hints of coffee and is very soft in the mouth.

Ingredients

  • 6 eggs.
  • 500g of mascarpone cheese.
  • 250g of cream.
  • Coffee.
  • Coffe liquour.
  • Around 50 ladyfingers.
  • 100g of sugar.
  • 25g of black chocolate.

Steps

  1. Put the egg whites and the yolks in separate bowls.
  2. Beat the egg yolks with 100g of sugar until the mix is clearer, nearly white in color.
  3. Make whipped cream with the cream (with no added sugar).
  4. Add the whipped cream, the yolks and sugar mix, and the mascarpone cheese and mix them until the resulting mix is homogenous. Add a small quantity of liquor to that mix.
  5. Beat the egg whites until stiff.
  6. Add together the stiff whites and the mascarpone mix and mix them carefully to form a foamy mix.
  7. Make about a liter of coffee and add some liquour to it.
  8. Carfully submerge, one at a time, the ladyfingers in the coffe and lay them in an flat container until they form a 'floor'.
  9. Add a cape of foam to cover the ladyfingers floor.
  10. Add another cape of wet ladyfingers and cover it with foam again. The goal is to have two layers of ladyfingers and a final covering of foam (as shown in the picture).
  11. Let it cool down in the oven at least 3 hours.
  12. Finley grate the cheese and spread it evenly on the tiramisu.
  13. Eat!
