Chocolate milk

Description

A perfect, not too sweet, chocolate milk to drink in the afternoon,

Ingredients

  • 10ml of cream.
  • 290ml of 3% milk.
  • 8g of chocolate powder (I use nesquick).

Steps

  1. Add 10ml of cream to a glass.
  2. Add 290ml of 3% milk to the glass.
  3. Mix the cream and the milk with a spoon.
  4. Add 8g of chocolate powder.
  5. Mix as much as you like (I usually prefer to mix so that some of the power is still undissolved in the surface of the milk).
  6. Drink!
